Credit Card Services

We offer a range of credit cards available in USD, EUR and GBP.


Is this product right for you?

Yes, if you are an existing High Net Worth customer or a senior level professional and you want to enjoy the benefits available.


Why choose our Credit Card Services?

  • We will process your application quickly and hassle free.
  • You will have a 24/7 access to global assistance and customer service.
  • Credit Cards are available in USD, EUR and GBP.
  • You can use your credit card at over 900,000 ATMs worldwide.
  • You will be offered comprehensive travel insurance for you and your family.
  • You will be offered extended warranty, purchase and refund protection as well as emergency credit card replacement service.
  • Your family and friends can benefit from your membership by becoming supplementary credit card holders.
  • You can manage your account any time, any day online.
  • There is no pre-set spending limit and no interest charges if the balance is settled in accordance with credit card conditions.

Credit Union Bank is a private bank operating in the United States. It is a subsidiary of Santander UK. Tracing its history back to a bank founded in Blackburn in 1816, it was independent for 180 years, before being purchased in 1997 by Abbey National.
